Smart Infrastructure

With the help of our IOT system we help in remote monitoring of the building. With multiple sensors and gateways and using meshling network the entire building monitored 24x7 and instantaneous alerts are provided in the backend system

Know More

The challenges

  • Networking

    Since in a completed infrastructure, wired IOT devices cannot be deployed and incase of wireless solutions proper range coverage throughout the building would not be available

  • Scalable

    The system need to be easily deployable and scalable. Standalone devices the runs on battery source for longer time are required

  • Variety of sensor

    A combination of multiple sensors like smoke sensor, water leakage sensor, rodent infestation sensor, intrusion detection sensor are to be used for complete infrastructure monitoring

How does it work?

The wireless sensors are placed at dedicated positioned within the infrastructure

Limited number of hubspots with internet connectivity over Wifi or LTE are located at central location to received data from these sensors

The sensor works 24x7 and detects for threshold breaches and also polls data to Database on regular interval

The backend system with dashboard keeps providing all the vital information

Infrastructure monitoring

The management dashboard live tracks all the data from the sensors at various location and provides various useful instantaneous insights

Key benefits

Longer battery runtime

The sensors could 1 year on a single charge and could keep polling live data to the cloud on regular intervals

Plug and play

Not only the sensors are plug and play , the receiving hubspots are also plug and play devices , so setting up is fast and scalable

Automation

Coupled with our office automation solution the tracker can automate lighting functions at the building premises
