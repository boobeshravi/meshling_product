Energy monitoring

With the help of IOT system We help enterprises in the energy space with energy monitoring and other insights thus improving overall productivity. We deploy mesh network enabled devices for complete energy mapping

Know More

The challenges

  • Networking

    Since the energy producing plant is spread over a large space, wired IOT devices cannot be deployed and at many cases proper data coverage throughout the plant would not be available

  • Scalable

    The system need to be easily deployable and scalable considering the fact the energy production cannot be halted deployment and maintenance of IOT systems

  • Longer life

    The devices are supposed to be robust since their supposed to be operating at adverse environmental condition

How does it work?

The Lora enabled devices with active energy monitoring feature are deployed in the field

The IOT devices in the field connect with each other forming a mesh network for relaying the information

The information from all the device are relayed to the main HubSpot connected to cloud and the data is pushed to the cloud database for further analytics

Energy monitoring

AI enabled backend system provide various insights like the estimated energy production and the conjunctive operational loss
